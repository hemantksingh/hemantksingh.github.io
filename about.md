### What I do

I'm the Director of R&D at [Finova](https://www.finova.tech/). My background is in technology modernisation and building and scaling engineering and data teams. I have delivered transformation programmes across financial services, the public sector and healthcare - experience that has taught me how to get teams delivering at pace while keeping governance and security front of mind.

Before Finova, I worked with SAAS companies in product development and with IT consultancies driving digital transformation, most recently as an Engineering Manager at Accenture, where I guided client executives through cloud migrations and tech strategy, and ran a security community of practice to develop cybersecurity skills across Accenture UK engineering.

I'm interested in creating environments where experimentation, learning, curiosity and a bit of bravery are encouraged. I also write and speak about designing, testing and securing software. Amidst rambling about stuff I also manage to write some code which is available on my [GitHub](https://github.com/hemantksingh) page. It has a bunch of things, from open source contributions to [what I am currently learning and experimenting](https://github.com/hemantksingh/messup-learn) with.
